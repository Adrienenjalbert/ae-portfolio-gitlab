Initiative 08 / 10 · Lifecycle · Retention · CRM

Lifecycle remarketing that drove 15x retention

A systematic re-engagement programme built on churn-risk segmentation and lifecycle-stage messaging, turning a leaky post-registration funnel into a retention and LTV engine.

Q2 to Q4 2024
MetaGoogle DisplayProgrammaticEmail automationCRM
The problem

Users registered, then churned

Post-registration churn was high and there was no systematic way to re-engage. Acquisition kept refilling a bucket that leaked from the bottom.

What I built

Segmented, staged re-engagement

I segmented users by churn risk, mapped messaging to lifecycle stage, optimised the channel mix across Meta, Display, programmatic and email, and ran win-back offers for dormant users, so the right message reached the right user at the right moment.

Architecture

How it ran

01 · Segment
Churn-risk model
Users grouped by likelihood and stage of churn.
02 · Message
Lifecycle-stage content
Messaging matched to where each user is in the journey.
03 · Mix
Channel optimisation
Meta, Display, programmatic and email balanced per segment.
04 · Win back
Reactivation offers
Targeted offers pull dormant users back.
