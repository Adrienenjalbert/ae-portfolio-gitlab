The problem

Scale peak demand without wrecking efficiency

Peak season pulls every lever toward volume. The challenge was to grow booked shifts hard while keeping cost per booked job under control, the opposite of what usually happens at peak.

What I built

Remarketing-first, lifecycle-led acquisition

Rather than buy net-new volume at any cost, I led with remarketing and lifecycle automation and governed the channel mix tightly, so the cheapest, highest-intent demand was captured first and new spend only scaled where it paid.
