The problem

Sales lived in manual research, not selling

Closing small clients was easy but not economically valuable. The large enterprise accounts that were worth winning ran 6 to 12 month cycles and closed under 5% won. Reps spent 60%+ of their time on manual research and contact discovery rather than selling, so the qualification work never scaled with the pipeline.

What I built

An agent pipeline that qualifies and prepares every account

I built a multi-agent system (known internally as "The Dude") that scores accounts against 20+ intent and firmographic signals, finds the real decision-makers with verified contact detail, and generates tailored, human-ready outreach. It writes back to Salesforce and notifies owners in Slack, so the output lands where the team already works.

It was designed around three ideas: precision targeting (surface the decision-makers most likely to convert and filter the noise), actionable company signals (real-time firmographic and behavioural data that shapes each message), and native tool integration (it lives inside the CRM and Slack, not a separate app). Strong enough adoption that it became part of the £45K Q1 2026 AI investment case.
