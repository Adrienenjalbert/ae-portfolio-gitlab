Initiative 03 / 10 · AI · CRO · Lead qualification

An AI-first chatbot that converts at 4.7%, against 1.9% on forms

I built 16 chatbots across the site, then an AI-first assistant with intent-based flows, live qualification and routing, and an A/B testing framework. It converts discussion-to-lead at 4.7%, against 1.9% for standard forms and the earlier bot fleet, because complex B2B buyers stall on static forms.

Feb 2025 to present
Custom chatbot frameworkClaude APIDynamic content engine
The problem

Static forms lost complex buyers

Standard forms converted at 1.9% and gave a poor experience to B2B buyers with real questions. Interest leaked away before qualification ever happened.

What I built

Conversational flows that qualify and route

The chatbots handle intent-based conversations, qualify against ICP in real time, and route to the right destination. An A/B testing framework runs each flow against forms and against the others, so the winners scale. A comparative analysis of AI-first versus traditional bots showed the AI-first flow drove more decisive, better-qualified conversations, and that is the one I scaled.
