A 5-agent content engine that took an article from £600 to £1

A multi-agent pipeline (research, outline, writing, CMS and QA) that produces brand-consistent SEO content at scale. It turned content from a bottleneck into a channel: 90% of B2B web traffic now comes from AI-generated content and lead magnets.

Feb 2025 to present
Multi-agent frameworkClaude APIWordPressSEMrush
The problem

Content cost too much and moved too slowly

B2B articles cost £600 each and took 5 to 7 days, capping output at 4 to 6 a month. Content couldn't keep pace with the demand-gen and SEO strategy that depended on it.

What I built

A pipeline of specialised agents with brand-voice control

Five agents work as an assembly line: a research agent gathers sources, an outline agent structures the piece, a writing agent drafts in brand voice, a CMS agent publishes to WordPress, and a QA agent checks quality and SEO before anything ships. Brand-voice guardrails keep 50+ pieces a month on-message.
