The problem

Acquisition was unsustainable and unattributed

A CPL of £1,072 made B2B acquisition uneconomic, and without clear attribution there was no way to know which channels actually drove revenue.

What I built

A governed, attributed multi-channel engine

I ran continuous multi-channel optimisation with intent-based bidding, per-channel landing pages and always-on creative testing, all wired to an attribution model, across an £11M budget. Spend followed evidence, not habit.
