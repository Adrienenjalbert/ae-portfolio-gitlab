01 Sales & revenue AI
Revenue AI OS

A multi-tenant Sales Operating System for B2B revenue teams

R&D / productised

The productised version of the sales-intelligence system that generated £440K of pipeline in production. Turns a CRM, calls and context into one self-improving research engine, so reps sell instead of search.

Why it exists

To prove the internal win generalises: a layered architecture (three product loops, a three-tier harness, a sales-knowledge layer) with ROI promises written into the spec and a documented process for adding tools, connectors and evals per tenant.

02 Content & SEO at scale
Content OS · your-os

A multi-tenant SEO content engine — one engine, many tenants

R&D / engine

The engine pattern behind the Career Hub: a closed content loop (discovery → strategy → brief → creation → approval) with two human-in-the-loop gates, pick-a-storage-mode per content type, and a pinned schema.

Why it exists

Built for a Growth/Performance lead who owns organic ROI: ship an SEO-driven content site without CMS lock-in, with quality gates that keep AI output on-brand. Ships with a 93/93 task suite and a documented audience and trust model.

03 Production programmatic SEO
Career Hub

The production Career Hub codebase

Production work

The statically-generated Next.js site behind the +286% organic clicks and +4,000% impressions: thousands of city×role pages, state calculators and interactive tools, built to AEO and Core-Web-Vitals standards.

How it is built

Feature-owned architecture, Biome + Vitest, and a Playwright-based smoke / Lighthouse / axe / visual-audit harness so quality is enforced, not hoped for. This is the engineering under the organic-growth case study.

04 Monetisation & data
LocalLens

A local-intent engine that monetises one data asset three ways

Live demoR&D

A config-driven local-SEO engine where a RevenueRouter decides — live, per request — whether a page earns through affiliate, CRM subscription or lead-gen, all inside a hard, code-enforced trust floor.

Why it exists

To model a CRISP-DM optimisation loop that activates at volume: one data asset, three revenue paths, a single router choosing the best. Static Next.js export plus Supabase Edge Functions, with CI/CD and a Vitest suite.
