    Simple to read, hard to fool.

    The generator recalls the evidence and recommends the test. A human still approves every launch. Three rules keep the loop honest.

    The human is the gate

    AI drafts and recalls. It never launches on its own. Every "save to second brain" is a person taking ownership of the design.

    Evidence quality decides

    A grade-D hunch can never overrule a grade-A holdout. Directional markets are flagged, so a shaky prior can't quietly shape a call.

    Every brief compounds

    Save one and it becomes reusable, graded knowledge. The next related goal recalls it in seconds, so the team stops repeating tests.
