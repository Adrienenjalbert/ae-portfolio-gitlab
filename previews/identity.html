Three ways off the AI-default look, without losing the blue

The current identity (warm cream #f4f1e8 + Fraunces serif + film grain) is close to the exact cluster AI portfolios default to; the cobalt accent is what rescues it. Each block below keeps cobalt as the signature and renders your real hero copy, KPI wall, a sample chart and quote, but moves the paper and/or the type off the default. Nothing here is wired to the live site. This complements directions.html (which explored dropping blue entirely).
